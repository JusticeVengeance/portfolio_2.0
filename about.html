I was born and raised in Spokane and after high school, I attended Spokane Falls Community College and completed a degree intended to transfer my credits to the Visual Communication Design Bachelors Degree at Eastern Washington University where I studied web development. After college, I worked in freelance development and eventually assumed a role as the Marketing Department's Front-End Web Developer at Washington Trust Bank. From my experience in that position, I grew a massive interest in marketing and began working with a variety of clients to grow their business using the emerging technologies in digital marketing. With a skill set focused in marketing and a strong foundation in web development, I found a position with a growing software company called CloudEngage. For several months I worked to develop drip campaigns, learning portals, and interactive web pages in an effort to streamline customer acquisition and growth for the company. At this point in my professional career, I am most invested in using my skills in marketing and development to grow as a part of a team of great people.
